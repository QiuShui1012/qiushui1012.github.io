# No Infinite Effects in HUD

No Infinite Effects in HUD (Nieih) is a client mod.
It will disable the display of the status effect in the upper right corner of the main HUD.

Whether the status effects are disabled depends on their duration and user configuration.

By default, this mod will only disable effects with unlimited duration.
You can disable effects based on whether the duration is infinite, or based on the classification of effects,
or even disable the entire mod.

These configurations can be modified within the game.
